Crystals Collector Game

Instructions


You will be give a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you
will add a specific amoung of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random
number, and you lose the game if your total score goes above
the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you
click on a specific crystal.

Each time the game starts, the values of each crystal will change.
